Staff

DDraceNetwork was an idea by Megaman with support by Delo and .me' in the summer 2013. Since the start deen manages the servers and develops DDraceNetwork. For new features or bugs talk about them in the Forum or suggest them on Github. New maps can also be submitted in the Forum, where our testers will take care of them. Please note the Mapper Rules.

Getting in Touch

You can often find us on the DDraceNetwork servers of course. Otherwise you can post in our Forum for suggestions, questions or anything else. There's also an IRC channel #ddnet on Quakenet (Webchat). deen can be reached by email at deen@ddnet.tw.
