Staff

DDraceNetwork was an idea by Megaman with support by Delo and onionly .me' in the summer of 2013. Since the start deen manages the servers and develops DDraceNetwork. For new features or bugs talk about them in the Forum or suggest them on Github. New maps can also be submitted in the Forum, where our testers will take care of them. Please note the Mapper Rules.
